Dear Hornby Dublo enthusiast,

I've been supplying Hornby Dublo spares to thousands of people with locos which weren't running properly since 2004, and running the mtrains website since 2008.

From those early days 16 years ago, people have actually found the website, and from a standing start I've sent out over five thousand invoices. But now it's time for me to reduce the range of items on this website, and move on to other project(s) to some extent - including actually running Hornby Dublo!

So I'm carrying on with quite a few of the stock items, but have reduced the range a bit, and won't be replenishing a few more of the items shown - so once those are gone, they'll be gone.

The website will continue to show what I have available, although the range of spares will reduce a bit with time.

As always, just email (john@mtrains.co.uk) with what you're after from the price list.

I want to thank all the splendid people who have had spare parts from me over the years.

John Holland (mtrains).
